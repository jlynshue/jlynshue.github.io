# Executive Workflow Business Strategy Pack

This directory is the internal strategy system for a personal-brand-led business sold through `jonathanlynshue.com`.

The business is intentionally narrow:

> I build AI workflow systems that remove executive reporting and coordination bottlenecks for CIO and ops-led teams.

Everything in this pack should reinforce that statement.

## Purpose

- Define a coherent business around a productized `Executive Workflow Sprint`.
- Narrow the current broad consulting positioning into a clear market wedge.
- Give future website, sales, and content work a stable source of truth.
- Prevent drift into generic AI consulting, broad agency work, or premature SaaS messaging.

## What This Business Is Not

- Not a generic AI agency.
- Not a day-one SaaS company.
- Not a broad development studio for any technical request.
- Not a content brand first and business second.
- Not a low-ticket freelance service business.

## Cross-Doc Rules

- Use the same ICP language everywhere.
- Use the same price assumptions everywhere unless intentionally revised in `00-business-foundation.md`.
- Keep the same CTA everywhere: `Schedule a Discovery Call`.
- Keep the same primary offer everywhere: `Executive Workflow Sprint`.
- Keep the same business wedge everywhere: recurring reporting, decision, or handoff bottlenecks across existing systems.
- Do not describe the business as "Fractional CIO. Edge AI architect. Full-stack builder." in future external assets without narrowing it to workflow outcomes.

## Review Cadence

- Weekly: review `07-validation-signals.md` against actual pipeline and content data.
- Monthly: update positioning, offer, and channel assumptions only if threshold criteria in `07-validation-signals.md` are met.
- Quarterly: decide whether to deepen the productized-service model or begin software productization work.

## Current Decision Snapshot

- Brand vehicle: `jonathanlynshue.com`
- Business model: productized service first
- Initial wedge: executive and ops workflow automation
- Offer structure: fixed-scope diagnostic and implementation sprint
- Primary market motion: personal credibility -> proof -> discovery call -> paid diagnostic
- Later option: reusable software modules only after repeated paid demand
