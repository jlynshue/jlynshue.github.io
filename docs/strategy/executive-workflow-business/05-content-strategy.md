# Content Strategy

## Content Goal

Build a compact body of work that proves three things:

1. you understand the buyer's operational pain
2. you can diagnose workflow failure better than generic AI consultants
3. you can turn that diagnosis into a paid engagement

## Editorial Position

This is an operator-led content program, not a media business.

Content exists to:

- create qualified demand
- sharpen your category
- support discovery and sales conversations

## Editorial Pillars

### Pillar 1: Workflow Bottlenecks

Focus:
- manual reporting tax
- coordination drag
- hidden steps inside recurring executive workflows

Purpose:
- name the pain clearly enough that the buyer self-qualifies

### Pillar 2: Systems Integration

Focus:
- why the stack is not the same thing as the workflow
- how M365, Jira, Confluence, and chat systems create fragmented decision flows

Purpose:
- show technical and operational fluency

### Pillar 3: Trustworthy AI In Operations

Focus:
- source-linking
- human review
- fallback paths
- trust over novelty

Purpose:
- differentiate from shallow automation work

### Pillar 4: Operator Proof

Focus:
- enterprise operating examples
- governance outcomes
- systems built
- lessons from real delivery

Purpose:
- justify why you can sell a premium narrow offer

## Content Formats

### Core Formats

- LinkedIn text posts
- LinkedIn carousel-style framework posts
- short essays or articles
- mini case notes
- offer-adjacent diagnostic explainers

### Supporting Formats

- checklists
- teardown posts
- simple framework diagrams
- annotated examples of before/after workflow logic

## Repurposing Workflow

1. Start with one strong operating insight.
2. Turn it into a LinkedIn post.
3. Expand the best-performing post into a short article.
4. Break the article into 2-3 derivative posts.
5. Convert the strongest framework into a lead magnet or sales asset.

This keeps the content system compact and compoundable.

## Publishing Cadence

### Monthly Baseline

- 12 LinkedIn posts
- 2 short-form articles
- 1 lead magnet or downloadable framework update

### Weekly Baseline

- 3 LinkedIn posts
- 1 repurposing action
- 1 sales enablement reuse

## Content Review Criteria

Before publishing, ask:

- Does this speak to a real buyer pain?
- Does it reinforce the workflow wedge?
- Does it signal operator credibility?
- Does it support the discovery call motion?

If not, it is probably noise.

## Content Standard

The content program succeeds when a qualified buyer can read three pieces and understand both the problem category and the first paid step.
