#### Key 2026 Shift
Buffer's 2026 data shows a significant shift: **late afternoon and evening hours (3–8 PM ET) now drive the highest LinkedIn engagement**, replacing the 2025 pattern of morning-only peaks. This aligns with executives consuming professional content after closing their laptops for the day.

### Recommended Posting Schedule for EWB

| Day | Time (ET) | Time (PT) | Post Type | Rationale |
|-----|:---------:|:---------:|-----------|-----------|
| **Tuesday** | **11:00 AM** | 8:00 AM | Operator Observation | Sprout peak; pre-lunch scroll window |
| **Wednesday** | **4:00 PM** | 1:00 PM | Framework / Teardown | Buffer's #1 slot; afternoon thought leadership |
| **Friday** | **3:00 PM** | 12:00 PM | Proof / Offer-Adjacent | Buffer's #2 slot; end-of-week reflection mode |

**Why not Monday?** Both Sprout and Buffer show Monday as the weakest day. Save your best content for mid-to-late week.

**Why not morning for all?** The 2026 data shows executives are shifting to afternoon LinkedIn consumption. The 11 AM Tuesday slot catches the pre-lunch crowd; the Wed/Fri afternoon slots catch the end-of-day strategizers.
