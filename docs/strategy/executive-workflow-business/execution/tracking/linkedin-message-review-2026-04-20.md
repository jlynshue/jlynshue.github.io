# LinkedIn Message Review — 2026-04-20

## Project Goals Summary

The `jonathanlynshue.com` website and LinkedIn presence serve a single business:

> I build AI workflow systems that remove executive reporting and coordination bottlenecks for CIO and ops-led teams.

**Offer ladder:** Discovery Call (free) -> Workflow Diagnostic ($2.5k) -> Executive Workflow Sprint ($12k) -> Workflow Tuning Retainer ($3.5k/mo)

**ICP:** U.S.-based CIO, COO, Head of Ops, PMO lead at operationally complex teams (50-2,000 employees) using Microsoft 365 + Jira/Confluence + Slack or Teams.

**LinkedIn role:** Primary demand channel. Proof-led, operator voice. Every interaction should reinforce the workflow category.
