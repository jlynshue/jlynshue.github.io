## Call Script Reference

### Opening (2 min)

"Thanks for taking the time. I'll keep this focused. I work with operations-heavy teams on a specific problem — recurring executive workflows that are still manually assembled across multiple tools. I'd love to understand what that looks like for your team."

### Core Questions (20 min)

| # | Question | What to listen for | Their answer |
|---|---------|-------------------|-------------|
| 1 | "What recurring workflow is most painful right now?" | Weekly readouts, status updates, decision briefs, handoffs | |
| 2 | "What systems are involved?" | M365, Jira, Confluence, Slack, Teams — more fragmented = better fit | |
| 3 | "How is it handled today?" | Manual assembly, copy-paste, someone owns it reluctantly | |
| 4 | "What does it cost in time, trust, or missed action?" | Hours/week, delayed decisions, low trust, dropped items | |
| 5 | "Who owns the outcome?" | Real person with authority (not "the team") | |
| 6 | "What would good look like in 30 days?" | Specific, measurable (not vague transformation) | |

## Close Options

### If Qualified (4–5 must-haves):

"Based on what you've described, this sounds like a strong fit for a Workflow Diagnostic. That's a 3-day paid engagement where I map the workflow, quantify the waste, and deliver a clear implementation recommendation. It's $2,500, and that fee is fully credited if you move to a sprint within 7 days. Would you like to explore that?"

### If Not Qualified (≤3 must-haves):

"Appreciate you sharing this. Based on what I'm hearing, [specific reason] makes this not the strongest fit right now. [Optional: suggest alternative or offer to reconnect later.]"

### If Ambiguous:

"I'd like to think on this and send you a brief note with my honest assessment of whether this is a fit. I'll follow up by [specific day]."

## Objection Quick Reference

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "We already have the tools." | "That's usually the point. The problem is the workflow between tools." |
| "We're figuring out our AI strategy." | "The sprint is narrow — no full AI strategy needed first." |
| "Can you just give us a roadmap?" | "The diagnostic delivers a decision-ready workflow spec, not a vague deck." |
| "Can this be bigger?" | "Start with one workflow so it can be measured quickly. Expand after." |
| "Budget is tight." | "The diagnostic is the budget-light way to reduce uncertainty." |
| "We've been burned by AI." | "That's why this is one workflow, source-linked, with clear success criteria." |
